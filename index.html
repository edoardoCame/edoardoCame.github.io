03 — Research & Projects

Selected works

01
December 2025 — January 2026

Persistence Analysis of Cointegration Relationships in Foreign Exchange Markets

Investigating the temporal stability of cointegration across 32 currency pairs (496 combinations) using 9 years of minute-level data with GPU acceleration. The study reveals that 65–80% of cointegrated pairs maintain their relationship the following day, with 90-day estimation windows providing significantly higher stability than shorter horizons.

Window90-day optimal vs. 14-day baseline
Beta Drift8–20% daily hedge ratio shift

Predictive Modeling of Explosive Volatility Regimes

A supervised ML framework for predicting explosive volatility regimes in AUD/NZD, designed to protect grid trading strategies from catastrophic drawdowns. Developed a Random Forest classifier on 90 engineered features achieving an AUC of 0.728 with a 12-hour prediction horizon.

Monte Carlo Validation of Mean Reversion Strategies

Validating the statistical robustness of a mean reversion trading strategy through 100,000+ simulated paths of EUR/GBP minute-level data generated via ARIMA time-series models. Implemented in Python with Numba and parallel computing for large-scale efficiency.
